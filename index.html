Our Mission

KEYES is a global movement to empower individuals—especially youth—to take charge of the planet's future through sustainability, innovation, and collective action.

Our Initiatives

  • KEYES Streets: Urban cleanups and beautification drives
  • KEYES Clubs: Youth sustainability teams in schools and colleges
  • Green Challenges: Monthly digital eco-actions
  • KEYES Circles: Local eco-ambassador networks
  • Starter Kits: Tools to begin your eco-journey

Join the Movement

Be a part of the change. KEYES invites everyone—students, citizens, organizations—to protect our planet and build sustainable cities together.
